The Story

Trevor is a reincarnator — someone who died in another world and was reborn with his memories intact. Lumiere is a regressor — a noblewoman who lived through a tragic timeline and was sent back in time to fix her mistakes. Their paths collide when Trevor, utterly oblivious to the romance novel plot unfolding around him, inadvertently derails everything Lumiere had planned for her second chance at life.

Part fantasy romance, part comedy of errors. Written and illustrated by Tobias Fong (Tanaka Tomoyuki).
