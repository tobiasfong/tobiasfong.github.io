About

The Story

A Warhammer 40,000 webcomic set in the cramped, stratified depths of Shorea Hive. Mikhail is a boy who dreams of exploring the stars — but in the 41st millennium, most people are born and die on the same world they never chose. He will be no different. Or so he thinks.

When tragedy strikes, Mikhail finds himself on an unexpected path — one that could see him rise through the ranks of a Knight Household, and closer to the stars than he ever dared imagine.
